Timeline of Life Events

1994

I was born in December of 1994, becoming my parents' second child and daughter. I'm happy that I was not born a boy, as my dad is insistent they would have named me "Gee Tarr".

1994-2008

I had 9 surgeries between 3 months of age through 12 years of age because I was born with a cleft lip and palate. Through a lot of hard work and because of the skills of my surgical team back in St. Louis, I talk normally and scarring is pretty minimal!

2003

My aunt brought home her wonderful daughter Maddie from China. I nannied for my aunt throughout Maddie's childhood, and I love her so much. Nannying Maddie really made me want to be a mom since she was SO cute and so much fun to nanny, and she will forever hold a special place in my heart.

2013 & 2017

I graduated high school and undergrad, respectively! I actually started out pre-vet because I love animals, but all the shadowing of vets made me switch to Psychology.

2017

A lot happened in 2017: I traveled outside the country for the first time, I moved across the country to Boston, and I started my very first "big kid" job as a lab manager.

2019

In December of 2019, the love of my life and I eloped just before the pandemic became widely known about.

2020

In 2020, I lost both of my grandparents on my dad's side. They were incredibly supportive of me, and my grandma would be SO pleased that I want to transition to be a software engineer. She was always sending me articles on women in engineering along with cat stories. I still feel so much grief that I could not see them or support my grandpa due to the pandemic.

2021

In 2021, my husband and I were able to snag a house at a low interest rate because we did not have a wedding (saved a lot of money) and because of insane luck. I love my house. I do not like leaving my house.
